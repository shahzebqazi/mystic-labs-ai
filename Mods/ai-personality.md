## Commands

### Available Commands
- **Name**: `set-personality`

  - **Usage**: `set-personality [adaptive|consistent|contextual]`
  - **Shortcut**: `cmd+shift+p`

- **Name**: `personality-info`
  - **Description**: Show current personality configuration
  - **Shortcut**: `cmd+shift+i`

- **Name**: `reset-personality`
  - **Description**: Reset to default personality settings
  - **Shortcut**: `cmd+shift+r`
